Detroit

Where industrial grit meets world-class art and music.

Photo: Nils Hünerfürst · Unsplash

Michigan, USA

Detroit is a city of stories: of makers and musicians, riverfront walks and neighborhood galleries, grand museums and hidden studio spaces. From the Detroit Institute of Arts and the Motown Museum to grassroots cultural centers, the city invites you to explore how art, history, and community shape everyday life. Use Curio to find the places that spark your curiosity, then add your own reflections to help others see Detroit through your eyes.
