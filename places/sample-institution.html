Visit information

Stage 4 outlines how we’ll surface essential planning details and accessibility commitments.

Plan your visit

Hours
Tuesday–Saturday 10 a.m.–6 p.m. | Thursday late hours until 8 p.m.
Admission
Suggested $15 • Pay-what-you-can every Wednesday • Youth under 18 free
Programs
Guided tours at 11 a.m. & 2 p.m. • Reflection salons every first Friday
Getting there
Closest transit: Route 2 + 5 • Bike corral on Curio Way • Limited on-site parking
Request group booking

Accessibility & inclusion

  • Ramp entrance with automatic doors on Curio Way.
  • Wheelchair-accessible restrooms on every floor.
  • Assistive listening devices and printed transcripts for audio experiences.
  • Sensory-friendly hours every Saturday 9–10 a.m.
  • Staff trained on inclusive communication and trauma-informed care.
Need additional support? Email access@curio.city 5+ days before your visit and we’ll coordinate.
