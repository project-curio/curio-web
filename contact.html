General inquiries

Email hello@curio.city for:

  • Product questions, beta signup, or feature feedback.
  • Press & speaking opportunities.

Institutions & partners

Email partners@curio.city for:

  • Curio for Culture onboarding.
  • Accessibility and interpretation audits.
  • Collaborative programming or licensing questions.
Please include your institution name, city, and availability for a 30 minute intro.

Access support

Email access@curio.city for:

  • Requests for alternative formats (large print, plain language, captions).
  • Sensory-friendly or ASL interpreted sessions.
  • Reporting an accessibility barrier in the product.
We’ll acknowledge within 24 hours and collaborate on a solution.
