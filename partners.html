What collaboration looks like

We keep onboarding lightweight and humane. Here’s the path every institution follows.

  1. Discovery session — share goals, audiences, access priorities, and internal workflows (60 minutes).
  2. Profile setup — send assets (logos, imagery, mission copy, access notes). We draft your Curio page.
  3. Reflection prompts — co-create guided prompts that align with your interpretive voice.
  4. Pilot launch — invite staff and community cohorts to seed reflections; we moderate together.
  5. Insights & iteration — review themes and plan programming or communications follow-ups.
